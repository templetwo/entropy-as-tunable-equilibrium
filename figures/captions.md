**Fig 1 — The τ-dial in kinetics (headline).**  
First-passage time distributions (to corridor midpoint x=0) for increasing horizon τ at fixed sep=6 (Corridor1D, causal force only). Distributions slide left and sharpen as τ grows. **Fig 2 — Engine vs readout.**  
Left: short-horizon (τ≈0.25) FPT distribution — a single curve (readout-like, effectively fixed). Right: the full engine τ-family (8 horizons). A readout has one distribution; the engine has a dial.

**Fig 3 — Reducibility is definitional in conservative 1D (not an empirical test).**  
Left: the measured FPT distributions vs τ (the dial). Right: explicit statement. Because Arm A showed the causal force is conservative (F_ce = −∇U_eff), at fixed τ the engine *is* equilibrium relaxation in U_eff(τ) by definition. In 1D every force is curl-free; "engine ≡ static U_eff" is a mathematical identity, not a discovery. Duplicate-SDE KS tests are circular and are not performed or claimed. The real empirical confirmation of thermodynamic equivalence is zero current (Fig 5 / Part D). A non-vacuous test exists only for non-conservative forces in 2D+ (v4 frontier). This run finds the negative: the τ-knob produces a dial, but that dial is exactly equilibrium dynamics in the τ-dependent U_eff. Ensemble 8000 per τ.

**Fig 4 — Engine first-passage scaling: drift-dominated (α ≈ 1).**  
Left: MFPT vs sep (log-log) at each τ. Right: fitted exponent α(τ) ≈ 0.7–1.1 (with CI; 9 sep points, 8000 traj/cell) plus reference α=1 (drift/ballistic under driven force). This is *not* the diffusive sep²/D (α=2) of thermal v2; force-driven transport produces α≈1 generically. Caveat (explicit in figure): α≈1 does not by itself separate the entropic engine from a disguised constant-reward force. The honest claim is narrower than "recovers the v1 signature." Data from manifest partC.

**Fig 5 — Positive control honesty anchor.**

Right: directional first-passage time asymmetry for the control (fwd vs rev rotation crossings of ±2π); KS p = 2.5e-5 (the actual measured value from the run). The instrument detects genuine NESS when present; the engine registers null (measurement, not blind spot).

**Fig 6 — U_eff(x, τ) surface.**  
Heatmap of the effective landscape U_eff(x, τ) = −βT_c S_c(x, τ) that the particle actually relaxes in at each horizon. Corridor wells at ≈ ±sep/2 (sep=6) marked. As the dial (τ) turns, the landscape reshapes, directly causing the FPT shift observed in Figs 1–3. (140-point x grid, 8 τ values.)
